Who We Are

A Movement Driven by Collective Action

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). Operating from Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, we address critical social issues through education, food distribution, women's empowerment, animal welfare, and environmental sustainability — across multiple Indian states.

We are powered by a dedicated network of volunteers and corporate partners, committed to bringing dignity, hope, and opportunity to every underprivileged individual we reach.

✅ Section 8 Registered ✅ 80G & 12A Certified ✅ CSR-1 Registered ✅ NITI Aayog ✅ ISO 9001:2015
What We Do

Our Ongoing Projects

Six flagship initiatives, each targeting a critical dimension of social welfare — from a child's first meal to a woman's first paycheck.

🍱
Project Seva

Hunger & Clothing Relief

Distributes nutritious meals and essential clothing to underprivileged communities. Beyond meeting basic needs, Seva fosters dignity and raises awareness about food security, ensuring no family goes hungry or suffers in extreme weather.

🔴 50,000+ meals & clothing distributed
📚
Project Bachpanshala

Education for Every Child

Bridges educational gaps by training underprivileged children in basic digital literacy, life skills, and school education support. Ensuring that poverty is never a barrier to learning and a brighter future.

🔵 Thousands of children empowered
👩‍💼
Project Udaan

Women's Empowerment

Soaring towards a brighter future — Udaan empowers women through skill development programs and financial independence initiatives, helping them become self-reliant contributors to their families and communities.

🟣 Financial independence for women
🌱
Project Prakriti

Environmental Conservation

Plant for a better tomorrow — Prakriti focuses on environmental conservation and sustainability efforts including tree planting drives, awareness campaigns, and initiatives that protect our natural ecosystems for future generations.

🟢 Trees planted, Earth protected
🐾
Project Jeev

Animal Welfare

Empowering lives, spreading compassion — Project Jeev focuses on rescue, protection, and feeding of stray and injured animals. Because a compassionate society cares not just for people, but for every living being.

🟠 Animals rescued & protected
🎯
Project Vikas

Skill Development & Employability

Enhancing employability through targeted skill development programs for youth. Vikas equips young individuals with market-relevant skills, helping them secure dignified livelihoods and contribute to India's growth story.

🟡 Youth trained for employment
Why It Matters

Social Impact of Our Work

Every initiative we run is a direct response to a real problem faced by real people across India. Here's why our projects matter.

🍚

Fighting Hunger

Millions of Indians sleep hungry every night. Project Seva ensures meals reach the most vulnerable families, restoring dignity alongside nutrition.

🏫

Bridging Education Gap

Poverty forces millions of children out of school. Bachpanshala provides learning spaces, digital literacy, and hope for a different future.

💪

Empowering Women

When women earn, families thrive. Project Udaan helps women break cycles of dependency through training and entrepreneurship support.

🌍

A Greener Tomorrow

Climate change disproportionately affects the poor. Prakriti's conservation work protects communities and the environment for generations ahead.
